ABOUT MONSTER KILLER

Monster Killer is an exciting turn-based battle game where you face off against a fearsome monster!

HOW TO PLAY

Use the following actions to defeat the monster:

  • ATTACK: Deal normal damage to the monster
  • STRONG ATTACK: Deal massive damage (limited uses)
  • HEAL: Restore your health points
  • SHOW LOG: View the battle history

You start with one bonus life. Use your moves wisely!
